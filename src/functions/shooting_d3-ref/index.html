American Mass Shootings 2018

Gun violence kills around 15,000 Americans every year (not including suicides) and leaves another 30,000 wounded. This visualisation show mass shootings - incidents where four or more people have been shot in one, not including the shooter. Mass shootings account for only a small proportion of total fatalities, but but the numbers are staggering.
